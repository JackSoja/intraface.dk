CARMAKOMA - HIGH FASION FOR CURVY GIRLS

Nyhedsbrev 01/August 2008












She’s got personality, curves, attitude. She likes the raw
style, but she also wants to emphasise her femininity.
In short, she is a woman who enjoys life and is not afraid
of showing that she values herself. But where does she
find the clothing that matches her curves and charisma?
– On carmakoma.com!

The new Danish designer brand Carmakoma is now ready
to launch an extensive collection in a feminine and sexy
style. The sizes range from 42 to 52, and this is where
Carmakoma goes against the mainstream by designing
clothing for the many curvy women who do not identify
themselves with the skinny and expressionless models
on the catwalk.

The fact that it is so difficult to find a proper range of
trendy clothing for curvy women seemed strange to
Angelica Weiss and Heidie Lykke. One day when, as
often before, they were discussing the fashion trade in
which they have both been working for many years, the
idea of Carmakoma arose, and from August their
clothing can be bought at carmakoma.com.

At long last there will be lots of options for curvy
women to get a cool, trendy style in clothing that fits
perfectly and emphasises their femininity – whether it
is for a night on the town or for everyday use, or just
whenever a little luxury is required.
